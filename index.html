Where We Dive

Kewalo Basin

Kewalo Basin is a vibrant boat harbor that is a popular destination for scuba diving. With crystal clear waters and a variety of marine life, the basin offers an unforgettable experience. The harbor is home to several dive such as the Kewalo Pipe, an artificial reef teeming with colorful fish and coral. Also, the basin's calm waters make it a great spot for beginner divers to gain experience. After a day of diving, visitors can grab a bite to eat at one of the many restaurants overlooking the harbor, or simply relax on the beach and take in the beautiful sights.

The YO-257

The YO-257 shipwreck is one of the most popular dive sites in Hawaii, offering an unforgettable experience for divers of all skill levels. This former Navy fuel supply vessel was intentionally sunk to create an artificial reef, which has now become a vibrant underwater environment. Diving the YO-257 is an incredible adventure, with chances to spot marine life, including sea turtles, schools of fish, and even the occasional shark. The wreck's location at a depth of around 100 feet adds to the excitement for new and experienced divers alike.

The Sea Tiger

The Sea Tiger shipwreck off the coast of Honolulu is a must-visit spot for divers. It's a former Chinese trading vessel that was intentionally sunk by the US Coast Guard, creating an artificial reef now home to marine life of all shapes and sizes. With a depth of around 120 feet, the wreck is perfect for experienced divers looking for a thrilling adventure or new divers looking for a challenge. This fascinating wreck site provides a unique opportunity to discover Hawaii's underwater beauty and experience the excitement of diving into a shipwreck.

Equipment

The equipment you will be given is a diving suit, which is a watertight costume for underwater use, connected to the surface or to a diving bell by a tube that provides the wearer with air. You will also be receiving an oxygen tank, which will determine how long your dive is! These will be given to each member of the group!
